DIRECTOR's MESSAGE

Prof. (Dr.) G.S. Virk joined NS College in july 2011. He superannuated from the Department of Botanical and Environment Sciences. He has more than 30 years of teaching and research experience. Prof. Virk did Ph.D from NS college in the year 1980 and joined as research assistant under Indian council of Agricultural sponsored project at the institute of Advanced Studies, Chaudhary Charan Singh College , Merrut. After serving for two years, Prof. Virk Joined as Head, Deptt of Biology, NS college, Batala and served their from june 1982 to may 1986. He joined the department of Botanical and Environment Sciences in may 1986 and serves this deptt. In the capacity of Lecturer, Senior Lecturer, Reader and Professor. During his tenure in the College he remained actively involved in teaching and research. He got several research Project from the funding agency like ministry of forests and Environment, College grants commission & council of Scientific and industrial research. He has more than 80 original research papers in the journals of national and international repute to his credit.

Apart from teaching and research, Prof. Virk served the College in the capacity of head and chairperson, Deptt. Of Botanical and Environment Sciences, Faculty Advisor, Deputy Director of all india Services, Science Project Manager, Courses Co-ordinator (Academic Staff Colleges), Chaired and Co-Chaired several academic sessions at conferences/ Symposia/ Seminars/ Workshops and was activity involved in various College activities like preparation of projects, College with Potential for Excellence in Particular Area (Life Sciences), prepa ration of annual research reports, compiled profile of NS College, compiled information for XI plan requirement of the College for submission to UGC, compiled and edited 40 years History of NS College in the form of a book, conceptual compilation of developmental activities of the College in the form of a book-Marching towards Excellence. Prof. Virk also served the College as Deputy Co-coordinator DRS (UGC) programme, deputy Co-ordinator for the the assessment of “College with Potential for Excellence” (UGC), officer Incharge Evaluation centers, and Director Hospitality.

Prof. Virk is on the subject expert panel of various Research Institutions, Universities, Selection Boards for the promotion and Selection of Scientists, Assistant Professor, Associate Professor and Professors. Prof. Virk was awarded National Biotechnology Associateship by Department of Biotechnology, Govt. of India and Senior Scientist for his excellent contributions in the field of Plant Sciences.
